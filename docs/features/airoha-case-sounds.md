## Case sounds

Enable or disable case sounds.

| Option | Description |
| ------ | ----------- |
| On     | —           |
| Off    | —           |

Place AirPods in the case, keep the case lid open and wait for the case light to turn off. Turn the option off/on, pull out AirPods while keeping the case lid open, wait at least 2 seconds and place AirPods in the case again, and wait for the case light to turn off.
